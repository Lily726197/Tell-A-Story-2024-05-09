The Story of John Wayne Gacy

John Wayne Gacy is one of the most influential serial killers of the 20th century. Known as the "Killer Clown" because he often dressed as a clown, Gacy is responsible for the murder of 33 boys and young men in the Chicago area during the 1970s. His pleading innocent because he was schizophrenic led to him being proved guilty of all the murders and his eventual execution via lethal injection in 1994. This is his story.
